Learning Front-End Development

Learning front-end web development involves understanding the structure of HTML, styling with CSS, and adding interactivity with JavaScript.

Each component of a webpage serves a purpose. HTML creates the content, CSS makes it look good, and JavaScript makes it interactive.

Why Practice is Important

Practicing consistently is essential to mastering front-end development. Regular projects and coding exercises help solidify your knowledge and improve your skills.
